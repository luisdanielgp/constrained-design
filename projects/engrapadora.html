The Souvenir Cotidiano collection was an effort from Tec de Monterrey students to create a series of objects under the typology of utilitarian object. They all featured ash wood, alluminum or brass in their construction. The final outcome was exhibited and sold at Zona Maco in Mexico City and Wanted Design in New York City.
